Yuqing Zhu is working as an assistant research professor in Tsinghua University, Beijing, China. Before, she worked at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences, Beijing, China. She received her PhD in Computer Science from Tsinghua University. Her research interests include databases, distributed systems, and large scale analytics.

Research Projects

  • ACIA Transactions (previously SHAFT): Transactions with Atomicity, Consistency, Isolation, Availability properties.
  • BestConfig: Automatically finding better configuration settings for a specific large-scale system under a given application workload.
