Yuqing Zhu

Assistant Professor

Advanced Computer System Research Center
Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences
Beijing, China

Yuqing Zhu is an Assistant Professor in the Institute of Computing Technology at Chinese Academy of Sciences. She received her PhD in Computer Science from Tsinghua University. Her research interests include databases, distributed systems, and large scale analytics.

Research Projects

  • RecodStore: Highly Available Datastore with On-Demand Trade-off between Replica Consistency and Response Time
  • Pronto (previously SHAFT): Highly Available Large-Scale Transactional Datastore
  • gShepherd: Big Data Store over Heterogeneous CPU Architecture
